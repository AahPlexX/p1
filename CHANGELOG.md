## [Unreleased]

### Added

- A complete, static, browser-playable RPG named *Ashen Vale*.
- Keyboard and touch exploration controls.
- Turn-based combat with strike, ember, guard, potion, and flee actions.
- Experience, levels, relic progression, a boss encounter, quest tracking, and recovery at the village.
- Local save persistence with safe recovery when browser storage is unavailable or invalid.
- A GitHub Pages deployment workflow and focused game-engine tests.
